Monkey Around IPA
-----------------
Target volume: 21 L
Target OG: 62
Target BU: 55

Ingredients:
------------
  3,5 kg Light Spray Malt Extract
  18 g Nugget hops pellets (13,4%)
  83 g Amarillo hops pellets (7,8%)
  61 g Cascade hops pellets (6%)
  11 g Nelson Souvin whole (11,5%)
  1 package Safale US-05
  1 ml Protafloc 
  
Boil 1 (20 minutes):
--------------------
Add 2kg of LME to the pot full of 10 L of cold water. Bring to boil and start timer.

Schedule:
  15 minutes: 0,5 ml Protafloc

Cool to 20 degrees and pour in fermentation bucket.

Boil 2 (60 minutes):
--------------------
Add 1,5 kg of LME to the pot full of 12,5 L of cold water. Bring to boil and start timer.

Schedule:
  60 minutes: 18 g Nugget hops
  15 minutes: 40 g Amarillo hops
              50 g Cascade hops
              0,5 ml Protafloc
    1 minute: 32 g Amarillo hops
              11 g Nelson Souvin hops

Cool to 20 degrees and pour in fermentation bucket.

Add yeast. Let yeast relax on top of fluid for 20 mins. Put on a lid and add an empty water lock to the hole. 
Shake the fermentation bucket heavily to aerate the fluid. Fill water lock with star san and wait.

Dry hopping:
------------
11 g Cascade hops
11 g Amarillo hops
