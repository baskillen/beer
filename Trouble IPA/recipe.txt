Target volume: 25 L
Target OG: 51,5

Ingredients:
------------
  3,5 kg Light LME
  25 g Chinook hops (11,9%)
  60 g Amarillo hops (9,6%)
  68 g Cascade hops (6%)
  1 package Safale US-05
  25 g Cascade (for dry-hopping)
  
Boil 1 (20 minutes):
--------------------
Add 2kg of LME to the pot full of 12,5 L of cold water. Bring to boil and start timer.

Schedule:
  20 minutes: 15 g Amarillo hops
              19 g Cascade hops
   5 minutes: 15 g Amarillo hops
              15 g Cascade hops

Cool to 20 degrees and pour in fermentation bucket.

Boil 2 (60 minutes):
--------------------
Add 1,5 kg of LME to the pot full of 12,5 L of cold water. Bring to boil and start timer.

Schedule:
  60 minutes: 25 g Chinook hops
  20 minutes: 15 g Amarillo hops
              19 g Cascade hops
   5 minutes: 15 g Amarillo hops
              15 g Cascade hops

Cool to 20 degrees and pour in fermentation bucket.

Add yeast. Let yeast relax on top of fluid for 20 mins. Put on a lid and add an empty water lock to the hole. 
Shake the fermentation bucket heavily to aerate the fluid. Fill water lock with star san and wait.
